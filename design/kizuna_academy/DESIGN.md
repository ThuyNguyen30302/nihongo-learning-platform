# Design System: Corporate Precision

## Vision and Strategy
This design system is built upon a foundation of trust, stability, and technical precision. Moving away from a vibrant magenta palette, the system now embraces a professional blue-centric architecture designed for high-clarity environments and enterprise-grade reliability.

## Color Language
The palette is defined by its deep, trustworthy tones and sophisticated accents.

*   **Primary (Navy/Trust):** #5471d1 - The dominant brand color, used for key actions and navigational elements.
*   **Secondary (Slate):** #6c759d - Provides a supportive, professional layer for secondary components and metadata.
*   **Tertiary (Brick):** #6c1901 - A high-contrast accent used sparingly for highlights or meaningful decorative elements.
*   **Neutral (Cool Gray):** #76767e - A balanced gray that ensures the interface remains grounded and legible.

## Typography
The system utilizes a modern, geometric typographic stack to emphasize clarity and technical proficiency.

*   **Headlines:** *Be Vietnam Pro* – A contemporary typeface that provides a clean, engineering-focused look for hierarchy.
*   **Body & Labels:** *Inter* – The industry standard for readability, ensuring that data-heavy interfaces remain accessible and clear.

## Shape and Space
*   **Roundedness:** Level 1 (Subtle). The corner radius is intentionally restrained to maintain a professional, sharp, and modern aesthetic without feeling harsh.
*   **Spacing:** Level 2 (Normal). The layout follows a standard density grid, balancing information density with comfortable negative space to prevent user fatigue.

## Intentionality
By transitioning to a "Fidelity" color variant based on a deep blue seed, the interface prioritizes a sense of institutional permanence and professional focus. The interaction model is designed to be crisp, reliable, and visually stable.
